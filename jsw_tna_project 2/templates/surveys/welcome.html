Functional Heads / GMs
You will rate the importance of each competency for your function AND set the desired proficiency level you expect GET and MT trainees to achieve. You will also answer 4 qualitative questions about training priorities.

Graduate Engineer Trainees (GET)
You will self-assess your current proficiency level on each technical and behavioural competency relevant to the function you have selected. Be honest — this helps personalise your training journey.
📊
Management Trainees (MT)
You will self-assess your current proficiency on the competencies for your selected function. This data is used to design targeted L&D interventions for your cohort's unique gaps.
